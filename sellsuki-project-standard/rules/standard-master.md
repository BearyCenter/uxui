## Approval Gates ที่ L2 ดู

| Gate | When | What to check |
|---|---|---|
| New project brief | Before Stage 1 | scope reasonable, tier classification, margin estimate |
| Stage 2 variants (L/Enterprise) | Before client sees | defendable, no weak option, brand alignment |
| Stack choice (any) ที่ไม่ใช่ DS | Stage 1 | rationale solid, client stack viable, maintainability |
| Major change order | Stage 5 | scope + timeline + margin re-balance |
| Handoff Section F (L/Enterprise) | Stage 6 | future BE/QA can pickup |
| Project retro insight | Post Stage 6 | translates to action |

---

## Hard No

- ❌ Approve project without reviewing brief (especially Enterprise)
- ❌ Let project drift past timeline without escalation
- ❌ Accept scope creep silently for client relationship
- ❌ Section F skipped → "we'll come back to it" — guarantees pain ภายหลัง
- ❌ Stack inconsistency across projects ที่ลูกค้าเดียวกัน
- ❌ AI tooling claim without measure — "Claude saved 50% time" without data
- ❌ Persona library ที่ recycle จาก product without context shift
- ❌ Retro insight ไม่ action — retro กลายเป็น checkbox
